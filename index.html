Impact of COVID-19
on the Street Art

The global pandemic has affected our lives on many levels, including our health, economic and social wellbeing. Along with the tourism sector, cultural and creative sectors are among the most affected by the current crisis. The venue-based sectors (such as museums, galleries, performing arts, live music, festivals, cinema, etc.) are the hardest hit by social distancing measures.

While the coronavirus pandemic forced almost the entire world to enter lockdown and people to stay home, street artists have found their way to bring urgent messages of hope. This website takes a look back at the last year in street art.
